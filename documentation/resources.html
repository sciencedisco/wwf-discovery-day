Resources

Your Discovery Day journey doesn't end here. Use these resources to showcase your work and continue learning.

Adding to Your CV

Here's how to effectively showcase your Discovery Day project on your resume:

  • Include the live URL of your GitHub Pages dashboard
  • Highlight the technologies you used (Python, Jupyter, GitHub Copilot, HTML/CSS, data visualization libraries)
  • Mention the skills you demonstrated (data analysis, AI-assisted coding, team collaboration, rapid prototyping)
  • Describe the impact - how your dashboard helps understand conservation challenges

Using in Interviews

Your Discovery Day project makes a great portfolio piece for interviews:

  • Show it live - walk through your dashboard during technical interviews
  • Explain your process - from data analysis to design to implementation
  • Discuss challenges - what obstacles you overcame and how you solved them
  • Highlight collaboration - how you worked as a team to deliver in one day

Continued Learning

Ready to deepen your AI-assisted coding skills? Check out these resources:

  • Microsoft APL (Applied AI Learning) Credential - Free certification in AI-assisted development
  • GitHub Copilot Documentation - Advanced techniques and best practices
  • Data Visualization Courses - Learn more about D3.js, Plotly, and other visualization libraries
  • Python for Data Science - Expand your pandas, numpy, and analysis skills
