# Droughts and Floods in Amazonia

**Source:** Marengo & Espinoza, 2016

## What this indicator measures

Overview of extreme hydrological events in the Amazon region, including major floods and droughts over recent decades.

## Key finding

Several extreme hydrological events, some characterized as 'once in a century', have been reported in the Amazon region during the last decade. Abundant rainfall has determined extreme floods along the river's main stem in 1953, 1989, 1999, 2009, 2012–2015. Deficient rainfall in 1983, 1995, 1997, 1998, 2005 and 2010 caused anomalously low river levels and an increase in the risk and number of fires.
