# Infrastructure Development in the Amazon

**Source:** Runde et al., 2020

## What this indicator measures

Overview of infrastructure development in the Amazon, including road, railway, waterway and telecommunications projects across Amazon countries.

## Key finding

Approximately 95% of all deforestation occurs within 50 km of highways or main roads in the Brazilian Amazon. In 2020, the Brazilian government has 69 infrastructure projects in the pipeline. A big new railway crossing Mato Grosso and Pará would increase export capacities of grains and minerals, increasing pressures on the Amazon and Cerrado.
