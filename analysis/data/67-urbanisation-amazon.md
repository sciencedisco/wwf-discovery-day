# Urbanisation in the Amazon

**Source:** Science Panel for the Amazon, 2021

## What this indicator measures

Assessment of the Amazon's population urbanisation trends and their implications for land use and livelihoods.

## Key finding

The Amazon's population overall is more than 60% urban. Amazonian livelihoods are increasingly a complex mixture of rural and urban activities. National and regional policies favour large-scale agribusiness, generating significant structural changes among Amazonian smallholders and increasing urban migration.
