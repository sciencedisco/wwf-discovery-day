# Agricultural Expansion in the Amazon

**Source:** Science Panel for the Amazon, 2021

## What this indicator measures

Overview of how agricultural expansion has proceeded across Amazon countries, including policy interventions and their effects.

## Key finding

In the early 2000s, large-scale cropland expansion occurred in the Brazilian Amazon. Conservation policies such as the soy moratorium and the creation of new protected areas attenuated soy-related deforestation. In Bolivia, soy is still expanding — the region of Santa Cruz has been identified as the largest deforestation hotspot in the Amazon, mainly due to forest conversion to soy fields.
