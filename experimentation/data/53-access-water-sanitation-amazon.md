# Access to Water and Sanitation in the Amazon

**Source:** Runde et al., 2020

## What this indicator measures

Assessment of gaps in infrastructure for clean water and sanitation across the Amazon region.

## Key finding

In the Amazon, there are noticeable gaps in infrastructure for basic services such as clean water and sanitation. In the Brazilian Amazon, more than 90% of people lack access to sewage collection and 40% lack access to water. 19% of people living in indigenous territories and 22% of people living in conservation areas lack access to electricity.
