# Soybean Expansion across South America, 2000–2019

**Source:** Song et al., 2021

## What this indicator measures

Analysis of the massive expansion of soybean cultivation across South America between 2000 and 2019.

## Key finding

The most rapid expansion occurred in the Brazilian Amazon, where soybean area increased more than tenfold, from 0.4 Mha to 4.6 Mha.
