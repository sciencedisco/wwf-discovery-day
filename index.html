DAY

Welcome

We kick off the day, followed by a short presentation from WWF on the research question and why it matters to them.

Get to Know Your Data

Teams of four, each with a dedicated coach, start with an icebreaker that doubles as a data exploration exercise: which data field do you identify most with, and why?

Design Your Product

Before touching any code, teams whiteboard what their final website should look like, using a catalog of chart types to shape their vision — maps, donut charts, heatmaps, interactive diagrams.

Analyse

Teams work through Jupyter notebooks, using GreenPT to qualitatively analyse the data and export their findings to CSV.

Build

Using GitHub Copilot, teams generate a single-page website hosted on GitHub Pages, based on a technical requirements file we provide and the product brief they created during the design phase.

Showcase and Borrel

Teams present their live websites on the big screen, then we celebrate with a borrel where everyone can walk around and explore each other's work.
